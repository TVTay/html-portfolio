The best Movies/Shows according to Taylor

My top 3 Movies/Shows of all-time

Young Royals

I love how it makes me the viewer understand their emotions and see all the forbidden love and drama between a prince and a townie.

The Finder

I like how the main character (the finder) is always able to find what he was looking for. It is quite interesting how he makes specific diagrams for every lost thing allowing him to figure out where about the item could possibly be.

Lost in space

This show made me fall inlove with space, I love how it mades a connection between a robot and human and how the robot protected the person after saving it. I personally find that nice.
